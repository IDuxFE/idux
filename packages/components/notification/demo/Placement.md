## zh

可控制通知消息弹出的位置，可选值有`topStart` | `topEnd` | `bottomStart` | `bottomEnd`。

默认为`topEnd`

## en

Can control the pop-up position of the notification message, optional values are `topStart` | `topEnd` | `bottomStart` | `bottomEnd`.

The default is `topEnd`.
